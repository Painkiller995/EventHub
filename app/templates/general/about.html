Our Story

EventHub was created in 2025 as a student project with a simple goal: make it easier for people to discover and join local events.

Our platform provides a simple yet powerful way for event organizers to create and manage events, while giving attendees an easy way to discover experiences that match their interests. Whether it's a small meetup, a workshop, or a large conference, EventHub provides all the tools needed to make your event successful.

How It Works

For Event Organizers

  1. Create a free account
  2. Set up your event with all details
  3. Publish your event to our platform
  4. Manage registrations and attendees
  5. Host a successful event!

For Attendees

  1. Browse events or search by interest
  2. Create a free account
  3. Register for events that interest you
  4. Receive email confirmations
  5. Attend and enjoy the event!
